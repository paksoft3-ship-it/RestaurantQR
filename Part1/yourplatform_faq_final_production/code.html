Does the Restaurant Need an Account?

arrow_forward

No. We handle everything. You never need to log in or manage software. We are a fully managed service designed to let you focus on your food.

Who Uploads the Menu?

arrow_forward

Our concierge team does. Simply email us your PDF, take a photo of your physical menu, or send us a link. We digitize, format, and optimize it for mobile viewing instantly.

How long does setup take?

arrow_forward

From the moment you sign up, your digital menu is live within 24 hours. Physical QR & NFC displays arrive at your location within 3-5 business days.

Can I update prices later?

arrow_forward

Yes. Just text or email your dedicated support agent with the changes. We update your digital menu immediately. No reprint costs required.

Getting Started

We provide a complete contactless menu solution. This includes high-quality, custom-branded physical QR & NFC displays for your tables, and a beautifully designed, mobile-optimized digital menu. We manage all the technical setup, hosting, and ongoing updates so you don't have to lift a finger.
Neither you nor your customers need an app. Customers simply tap their phone on the NFC tag or scan the QR code using their standard camera app. The menu opens instantly in their mobile browser. For restaurants, there is no dashboard to manage—you communicate updates directly with our team.

QR & NFC Hardware

Our standard displays are built to withstand heavy restaurant use, including spills and wiping. However, if one breaks or is lost, simply contact us. We offer affordable replacements shipped next-day.
