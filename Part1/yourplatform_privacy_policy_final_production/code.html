1. Overview

Welcome to YourPlatform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our digital hospitality services. Our primary goal is to provide a seamless ordering and dining experience while respecting your privacy.

Importantly, our platform is designed to function largely without requiring user registration. You can browse menus and interact with restaurant services anonymously where permitted.

4. Information Collection

We minimize data collection to what is strictly necessary. We may collect:

Restaurant Business Data

When you place an order, we collect details necessary to fulfill it: items selected, table number (if applicable), and transaction total.

Contact & Enquiry

If you contact support or request a receipt, we collect your email address or phone number solely for that interaction.

Technical Data

Device type, browser version, and basic location data (city level) to optimize menu rendering and language preferences.
