1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website or use our services. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to the owners of the site.

We use cookies to ensure the technical functioning of our platform, remember your preferences (such as dietary requirements or location), and analyze how our services are used so we can continuously improve.

2. Cookie Categories

We classify the cookies we use into the following categories:

  • Essential (Strictly Necessary): These cookies are required for the operation of our platform. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas or use a shopping cart.
  • Preference (Functional): These are used to recognize you when you return to our platform. This enables us to personalize our content for you and remember your preferences.
  • Analytical/Performance: They allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors and to see how visitors move around our platform when they are using it.
  • Marketing (Targeting): These cookies record your visit, the pages you have visited, and the links you have followed. We will use this information to make our platform and advertising more relevant to your interests.

3. Functional Integrations

Our service integrates with third-party providers for delivery, payment processing, and mapping services. These partners may place cookies on your device to facilitate these services. We ensure all partners comply with relevant data protection regulations.

4. QR/NFC Interaction Information

When you interact with our in-store QR codes or NFC tags to view menus or place orders, a temporary session cookie may be placed on your device to maintain your order state. These are typically session-based and expire shortly after your visit.
